Course Outline

This program features four modules, including live classes:

Week - 1

Module 1 Introduction to Microorganisms

In the first section, you will gain a thorough introduction to the course, microorganisms as an agents of disease and their impact on human health and economic value of microorganisms including the materials that will be covered, the knowledge you are expected obtain, and the contributions that will help you get the most out of the course.

+
Week - 2

Module 2 Microorganisms and Human Health

In this section, we will look into Understanding of the human microbiome, Role of microorganisms in maintaining a healthy immune system, Microorganisms as a sources of antibiotics and other therapeutic substances and Applications of microbiology in medicine and biotechnology

+
Week - 3

Module 3 Microorganisms and Environmental Sustainability

In this section, we will understand Microorganisms in nutrient cycling and soil fertility, Microbial degradation of pollutants and Waste management, harnessing microorganisms remediation and bioremediation production and Microorganism in Sustainable agriculture and food production

+
Week - 4

Module 4 Microorganisms and future

In the Last section, we will look at the future of microorganisms as regards to wealth and health
